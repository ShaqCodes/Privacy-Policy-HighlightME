HighlightME Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/27/24

1. Introduction

HighlightME ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Website Content: The text you highlight on web pages.
  • Web History: The URLs and titles of pages where you create highlights.
  • User Activity: Your interactions with the extension, such as creating, editing, or deleting highlights.

We do not collect personal information, financial information, health information, authentication information, personal communications, or precise location data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve the HighlightME service. This includes:

  • Storing and displaying your highlights
  • Allowing you to search and organize your highlights
  • Improving the functionality of the extension

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and, if you're signed into Chrome, synced with your Google account using Chrome's built-in sync functionality. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time through the extension interface.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the extension. You can delete your data at any time through the extension settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the extension.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at highlightme1848@gmail.com.
